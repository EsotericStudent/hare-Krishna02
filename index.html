About the Hare Krishna Movement

The Hare Krishna movement in Norway started in Bergen

Teachings

1. Dharma (Righteous Duty and Universal order)

2. Yoga (Union)

3. Detachment and Selflessness

4.Equanimity

5.Knowledge and Wisdom

6.Devotion to God

7.Discipline and Self-Control

8.Renunciation of Fruits of Actions

These teachings collectively provide a comprehensive guide for leading a life of purpose, righteousness, and spiritual growth, addressing the challenges and dilemmas that individuals may encounter on their journey.

7.Preaching and outreach
